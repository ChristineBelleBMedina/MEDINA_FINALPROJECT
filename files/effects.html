WHAT ARE ITS EFFECTS?
Numerous are not that aware, but poverty really has power in terms of its effects since it has brought several consequences in the society.

These effects may be short term or long term, depending on the situation. However, some are really long lasting and are difficult to eradicate. These may also be detrimental, especially to the youth and their future
BELOW ARE THE EFFECTS OF POVERTY AND THEIR ROOTS:

MENTAL HEALTH ISSUES

Individuals who experience poverty, face a lot of stress and struggles in life.
It can affect and worsen one’s mental health, leading to depression, traumas and such.

PHYSICAL HEALTH ISSUES

Poverty can really affect one’s physical health. Individuals may develop serious diseases
due to the limited access only of proper health care. It may be due to financial instability which became a barrier for them to get the daily necessities in life causing their health to be at high risk.

POOR QUALITY OF EDUCATION

Individuals living in poverty experience poor quality of education. This may be due to financial instability also and the lack of resources in education such as books and expenses in school. Some cannot even go to school since poverty’s power can really limit one’s access to education.
