WHAT ARE ITS PREVENTIONS?

With poverty, there is power. It is not always about the negative side such as its substandard effects, there is also a much brighter side which are its preventions. There is power in poverty and that is its preventions.
Prevention is always better than cure.

BELOW ARE THE PREVENTIONS OF POVERTY:

GENDER EQUALITY

You may not know but gender equality can reduce poverty over time. As it gives both genders the equal access to opportunities and qualities in life, increasing the economic growth which can lessen poverty in the society. It also eradicates gender inequality at the same time as it is one of the roots for poverty.

EDUCATION

This is one of the most significant factors in preventing poverty. There is really power in education as it can reduce poverty in several ways. Increasing access to education leads to much higher opportunities for individuals. Education is really interconnected with economic growth as it serves as a door to the individuals to get out of poverty.

INCREASING ACCESS TO
FOOD, SANITATION AND HEALTH CARE

Increasing access to food, sanitation and health care can prevent poverty since it only shows that the individuals have attained enough support from the society. It also entails how significant the
quality way of living is .
