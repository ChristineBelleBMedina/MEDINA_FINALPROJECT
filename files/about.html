WHAT IS POVERTY?

Nowadays, the rate of poverty here in the Philippines is rapidly increasing. Many individuals have been suffering with the said issue. Some lacks financial and essential resources, resulting them to not achieve the basic necessities in our daily lives. However, poverty is not just about not having enough money, it is more than that.


POWER-TY addresses certain issues regarding poverty. It aims to spread awareness on what poverty is and how powerful both its effects and preventions. It correlates to the first SDG, "No poverty" since it is for the individuals to know and to be more aware of the effects of poverty so that they can seek preventions to it.
